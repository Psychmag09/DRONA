Major External Factors

  • Train Speed

    As the train speed increases,Dynamic load also increases thereby resulting in change in frequency.Speed of train, speed of sound in rail and natural frequency taken into into account to calculate the frequency varation.

  • Temperature

    As the temperature rises, the Frequency Variation also rises. Coefficient of Thermal Expansion Of Rail,Length of Rail and Change in Temperature are taken into account to calculate the frequency varation.

  • Track Curvature

    More the curvature less is the frequency variation. Speed of train, Radius of Curvature of the Track and Natural frequency of the Rail Track are taken into account to calculate the frequency varation.
